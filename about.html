ABOUT

A virtual book club - full of suggestions, reviews and inspiration for 2020

I love reading but somehow don’t fit it into my busy schedule. It’s a bit like exercise - I need some sort of structure or it’s never going to happen. And I don’t think I’m the only one. That is why I’m setting up Lucy Reads, a virtual book club.

The format is easy - I will recommend one book a month. Feel free to read it in your own time or add it to your list. When you’ve read it, you can rate the book or send me your thoughts. At the end of the month, I will post my review and a summary of the reviews I’ve received from other book club members.

The books will all be recently published and often recommended by friends, celebrities or authors themselves. Hopefully Lucy Reads will encourage you to read more, enable you to keep up to date with current literature and help you to take some time for yourself. Please follow @lucyreads2020 on Instagram for updates!
