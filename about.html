ABOUT

A virtual book club - full of suggestions, reviews and inspiration

Last year, I decided that my new year's resolution was to read more. I used to love reading but somehow could never find time to fit it into my busy schedule. That is why I set up Lucy Reads, a virtual book club. Lucy Reads allowed me to explore new literature that I definitely would not have read otherwise, and to share it with others who wanted to join me. I've loved reading all your reviews and hearing your thoughts so I have decided to continue with Lucy Reads into 2021!

The format is easy - I recommend one book a month. Feel free to read it in your own time or add it to your list. When you’ve read it, you can rate the book or send me your thoughts. At the end of the month, I post my review and a summary of the reviews I’ve received from other book club members.

The books are all be recently published and often recommended by friends, celebrities or authors themselves. Hopefully Lucy Reads will encourage you to read more, enable you to keep up to date with current literature and help you to take some time for yourself. Please follow @lucyreadsbookclub on Instagram for updates!
